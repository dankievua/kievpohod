ARTWORKS

Photography and color correction

In 2012 [29] (after 8 years of photography practice) I began experimenting more with color's influence to the perception and the possibility of very accurate photo (and later video) digital color correction. I have worked more with the photos of the urban landscape and else with girl's photosets.

It should be clear that you need a good and color calibrated monitor to fully experience these photos. 


There are more photos in my profile on 500px.com
